## ✨ What Makes Us Different

- **Mobile app** - Manage agents from your phone
- **ZK proofs** - Private payments with verification
- **ML risk scoring** - AI detects suspicious transactions
- **Multi-chain** - Works on Stellar and EVM chains
- **Real-time oracle** - Live prices from Binance, CoinGecko, Kraken

---

## 🏗️ How It Works

Human deposits funds into agent

Human sets spending limits (daily, weekly, monthly)

AI agent pays autonomously within limits

ML model scores risk in real-time

ZK proof verifies payment without revealing secrets

Oracle ensures fair pricing

All recorded on Stellar blockchain
